Cheddar Parmesan Scalloped Red Potatoes

Prep Time: 20 minutes | Cook Time: 50 minutes | Total Time: 1 hour 10 minutes

Yield: 6 servings

Ingredients:

  • Red Potatoes (sliced thin) 1 1/2 lb
  • Unsalted butter 3 tbsp
  • Flour 3 cup
  • Dried garlic 1/2 tsp
  • Salt 1/2 tsp
  • Pepper 1/8 tsp
  • Milk 2 cup
  • Cheddar Cheese 1 1/4 cup
  • Parmesan Cheese 2/3 cup

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 400ÂºF.
  2. Melt the butter in a sauce pan over medium heat until it is beginning to bubble, add the flour and the spices. Mix well and cook until the mixture has bubbled for one minute.
  3. Reduce the heat to low, slowly add in the milk, stirring constantly, so that a sauce forms without lumps. Heat to medium and bring the mixture up to a gentle simmer for one minute, then remove it from the heat.
  4. Stir in the shredded cheddar and parmesan cheese until evenly combined.
  5. Lightly grease a 9x13 pan with butter, arrange half of the potatoes along the bottom. Evenly coat the potatoes with half of the creamy sauce. Arrange the rest of the potatoes and coat with the remaining sauce.
  6. Bake for 45-55 minutes

Sweet Potato Casserole

Prep Time: 20 minutes | Cook Time: 45 minutes | Total Time: 1 hour 5 minutes

Yield: 10 servings

Casserole Ingredients:

  • Sweet Potatoes 4 cup
  • Large Eggs 2
  • Sugar 1/2 cup
  • Milk 1/2 cup
  • Butter (softened) 4 tbsp
  • Vanilla Extract 1/2 tsp
  • Salt 1/2 tsp

Topping Ingredients:

  • Brown Sugar 1/2 cup
  • Flour 1/3 cup
  • Butter (softened) 3 tbsp

Instructions:

  1. Cook and mash the sweet potatoes, add eggs; mix well. Add sugar, milk, butter, vanilla, and salt; mix until smooth. Transfer to a 9x13-inch baking dish.
  2. To make the topping: Mix brown sugar and flour together in a medium bowl. Cut in butter with a pastry cutter until mixture is coarse and looks like peas; don't overmix. Sprinkle topping over sweet potato mixture.
  3. Bake in the preheated oven until topping is lightly browned, about 30 minutes.
